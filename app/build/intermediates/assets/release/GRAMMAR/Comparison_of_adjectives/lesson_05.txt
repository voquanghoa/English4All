2.The road was ____ (long) than we had expected.
longer

3.The girl is very ____ (intelligent).
intelligent

4.As soon as they understood that the second book was ____ (boring) than the first one, they changed their mind.
more boring

5.The shop is ____ (far) than the school.
further

the furthest

7.Tim is ____ (intelligent) than Pat.
more intelligent

8.The heat is less ____ (harmful) than the cold.
harmful

9.This song is ____ (beautiful) one in the world!
the most beautiful

10. They are as ____ (stubborn) as donkeys!
stubborn
